Frequently asked questions

What does Cypaction build?

We're an Australian mobile app development company. We build iOS and Android applications using Flutter, with a focus on location-aware, community-driven apps for Australian users.

When will your apps be available?

Both AusTravla and MarketStalls are in active development. We don't have a confirmed public launch date yet. Contact us to register interest and we'll be in touch when they're ready.

Do you take on contract development work?

At this stage, Cypaction is focused on building its own products. If you have a specific enquiry, feel free to email us and we can discuss further.

How do you handle personal data?

We take privacy seriously. Our company-level Privacy Policy is available at cypaction.org/privacy.html. Each app will have its own dedicated privacy policy once launched.

Where is Cypaction based?

Cypaction Pty Limited is an Australian company. We operate under Australian law, and all personal data we collect as a company is handled under the Australian Privacy Principles.

Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes. Under the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of personal information we hold about you. Email hq@cypaction.org to make a request. See Privacy Policy §8 for full details.
