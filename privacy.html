1. Who we are

Cypaction Pty Limited (ACN 008 650 664, ABN 22 008 650 664) ("Cypaction", "we", "us", "our") is an Australian mobile application development company.

We are committed to handling personal information responsibly and in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) in Schedule 1 of that Act.

Cypaction may in the future transfer the operation of individual apps to wholly-owned subsidiaries. If that occurs, those subsidiaries will publish their own privacy policies covering their specific apps and data handling. This policy covers Cypaction Pty Limited as the corporate entity only.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to personal information collected by Cypaction Pty Limited through:

  • Our company website at cypaction.org (and any associated subdomains)
  • Direct business communications with us — including enquiries, support requests, and contractor or supplier contact
  • Our general business operations as a mobile app development company

This policy does not cover the individual mobile applications we develop (such as AusTravla or MarketStalls). Each application will have its own privacy policy published on its own website, governing how personal information is collected and handled within that app.

This policy is written in Australian English and governed by Australian law. We do not apply foreign privacy frameworks (such as the GDPR or CCPA) by default.

3. What personal information we collect

In operating our company website and running our business, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact details — name, email address, and any other information you voluntarily provide when contacting us via our website or email
  • Business contact information — name, business name, email, and phone number of contractors, suppliers, and professional contacts
  • Correspondence — the content of emails or messages you send us
  • Website usage data — standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time of visit) collected automatically when you visit our website

We do not collect sensitive information (as defined in the Privacy Act 1988, including health information, racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, or biometric data) through our website or business communications. We do not operate advertising technology, behavioural tracking, or user profiling on our website.

4. How we collect personal information

We collect personal information:

  • Directly from you — when you contact us via our website contact form, email, or other direct communication
  • Automatically — standard web server logs when you visit our website (see section 3)
  • In the course of business — when engaging contractors, suppliers, or professional service providers

We do not purchase personal information from third-party data brokers.

5. Why we collect personal information

We collect and use personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected, or directly related purposes you would reasonably expect (APP 6). Those purposes are:

  • Responding to your enquiries and support requests
  • Managing our business relationships with contractors and suppliers
  • Operating and improving our website
  • Communicating with you about our company and the products we are developing, where you have expressed interest
  • Complying with our legal obligations

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We do not display advertising on our website.

7. How we protect your information (APP 11)

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Our measures include:

  • Encrypted transmission of all website communications (HTTPS)
  • Access controls limiting who within Cypaction can access personal information
  • Using reputable, security-conscious third-party service providers

When personal information is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was collected, and we are not required by law to retain it, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it.

8. Your rights — access, correction and deletion (APP 12 & 13)

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you (APP 12), and to request correction of information that is inaccurate, out of date, incomplete, or misleading (APP 13).

You may also request that we delete personal information we hold about you, where we are not required by law to retain it.

To make any such request, contact us at hq@cypaction.org. We will respond within 30 days. We will not charge you for making a request. You can also visit our Support page for further guidance.

9. Data breaches

If we become aware of a data breach that is likely to result in serious harm to any individual whose personal information is involved, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as soon as practicable, in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme under Part IIIC of the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be published at cypaction.org/privacy.html. The date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last updated. Material changes will be notified on our website.

11. Complaints

If you have a concern or complaint about how we have handled your personal information, please contact us first at hq@cypaction.org. We will acknowledge your complaint within 5 business days and aim to resolve it within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):
